+
BI tools show you what happened. Aitora shows you why tracing the specific decision, months earlier, that caused what you're seeing now. They're complementary. You keep your dashboards. Aitora is the causal intelligence layer on top.

+
Read-only API access to your existing tools like CRM, HRIS, finance, product analytics. We never write to your systems. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in your region, and never used to train models. Your data stays yours.
